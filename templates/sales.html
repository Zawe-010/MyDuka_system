Welcome to the Sales Page. This page allows you to track and record product sales efficiently. Each sale entry is logged with a reference to the product sold, the quantity, and the date it was created.

To record a new sale, click the "Add Sale" button. A modal will appear where you can:

  • Select a product from the dropdown list of existing inventory.
  • Enter the quantity of the product sold.
Once submitted, the sale is automatically added to the table below for your records.

The sales table provides a real-time overview of all recorded transactions, including:
  • Sales ID: A unique identifier for each sale.
  • Product ID: References the product sold.
  • Quantity: Number of units sold.
  • Created At: Timestamp of when the sale was recorded.
Use this page to maintain accurate sales records, analyze trends, and ensure seamless inventory updates.
